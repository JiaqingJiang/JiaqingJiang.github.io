About

I am a PhD student at Caltech, advised by Thomas Vidick. I am broadly interested in quantum information and theoretical computer science. Recently I am interested in Hamiltonian Complexity, understanding algorithms/hardness for computing ground energy and constructing ground states.

I am pretty interested in finding tracatble cases of local Hamiltonian problem, or identifying "natural" property which will make local Hamiltonian problem easier than QMA. It's well-known that in general Local Hamiltonian problem is QMA-hard, but it will be super cool if we can say for some subcases, a quantum algorithm can solve it!
